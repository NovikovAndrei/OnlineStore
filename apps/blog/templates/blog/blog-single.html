Interesting Pizza Facts

1. Square pizza

The shape of the original Italian pizza was square. Today it is better known as "Sicilian". At first, Romano cheese was also used to make pizza, and then they switched to mozzarella, beloved by many today.

2. The average slice of pizza is 25% protein.

This was reported by the US Department of Agriculture. In addition, pizza provides 35% of the daily protein intake.

3. One of the first documented online purchases was pizza.

Pepperoni with mushrooms and extra cheese. It was one of the first recorded purchases made online around 1994. More purchases are now made online than any other way.

4. Pizza Eating Record.

In 2006, Cristian Dumitru from Romania set a new world record by eating 90 kg of pizza in a week. Another record belongs to the Japanese Takeru Kobayashi, who in 2014 ate 62 slices of pizza (15 and a half pizzas) in 12 minutes.

5. Pizza Margherita was named after Queen Margherita of Italy.

Raffaele Esposito, a pizza maker, was commissioned by the Royal Palace to create a pizza in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy. Of the three different pizzas he made, the queen's favorite was the pizza in the colors of the Italian flag - red (tomatoes), green (basil) and white (mozzarella).
