Returns & Exchange.

Returns Policy:
If the products you received do not meet your expectations or have any defects, please contact our customer support within 5 hours of receiving your order. We will review your claim and provide you with instructions for returning the items.

Submitting a Request:
To request a return, please contact our customer support at the phone number provided on the website or send an email to the specified address. Include the following information in your message: order number, product details, reason for return, and your contact information.

Processing Requests:
We strive to process return requests as quickly as possible. After receiving your request, our team will contact you for confirmation and further instructions. Please note that returns may take some time to process, and refunds will be issued to the same payment method used for the original purchase.

We value your trust and aim to make your journey into the world of Italian cuisine enjoyable and satisfying. If you have any questions or need additional information about our returns policy, please contact our customer support. We are always ready to assist you.
