Shipping Information

At our authentic Italian cuisine delivery service, we strive to provide a seamless and convenient shipping experience for our customers.

Here is some essential information regarding our shipping process:

Delivery Area:
We currently offer delivery services within the St-Petersburg area. Please ensure that your delivery address falls within our designated delivery zone before placing an order. If you have any questions about the delivery area, feel free to contact our customer support team.

Delivery Time:
We understand the importance of timely delivery. Our dedicated delivery team works diligently to ensure that your order reaches you as quickly as possible. The estimated delivery time will be provided to you during the checkout process. However, please note that delivery times may vary depending on factors such as order volume and traffic conditions.

Delivery Charges:
We offer free delivery on orders over $30. For orders below this threshold, a nominal delivery fee may apply. The exact delivery charges will be displayed at the time of checkout. We believe in transparency, and there are no hidden fees or additional charges involved in our shipping process.

Thank you for choosing our authentic Italian cuisine delivery service. We look forward to serving you and bringing the taste of Italy to your doorstep with our delicious dishes and reliable shipping services.
