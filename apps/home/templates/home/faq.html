Frequently Asked Questions about Delivery

How do I place an order for delivery?

You can place an order for Italian food delivery on our website. Simply choose dishes from our menu, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in the necessary information, select a convenient delivery time, and then confirm your order. We will be happy to deliver your Italian food right to your door.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods to make the ordering process as convenient as possible for you. You can pay for your order online using credit or debit cards, as well as through payment systems such as PayPal or Apple Pay. Additionally, we also accept cash payment upon delivery.

What should I do if I want to modify or cancel my order?

If you wish to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer support as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request. Please note that if your order is already being prepared or in transit, we may limit the ability to make changes or cancellations.

If you have any other questions, please contact our customer support. We are always ready to assist you and make your culinary journey into the world of Italian cuisine enjoyable and convenient!
